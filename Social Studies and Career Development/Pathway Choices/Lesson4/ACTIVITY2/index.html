Build Your Tower of Success!

Ready to build your future? Each block you place represents a benefit of choosing the right learning pathway. But choose wisely. If the statement is not true, your tower might shake or even tumble! Let's stack up those advantages and build a solid future together!

Learner Instructions

  • Read each block statement at the bottom of the screen.
  • Decide if it is a true benefit of choosing a pathway.
  • If it's true, drag it to your tower to stack it up.
  • If it's false, adding it will make your tower wobble and lose stability!
  • Watch your Stability Score. The more correct blocks you place, the stronger your tower becomes.
  • Win by stacking all the correct benefit blocks to complete your tower!
