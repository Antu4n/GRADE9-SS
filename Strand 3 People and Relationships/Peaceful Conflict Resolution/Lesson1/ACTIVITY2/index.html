Activity Introduction

Welcome to Harmony Town! All across this community, people are finding smart and kind ways to live together peacefully. Your task is to click on different places in the town to discover how peace is put into action in real-life spaces, just like in your own community. Ready to explore?

Learner Instructions

  • Look at the map of Harmony Town.
  • Click each glowing hotspot to reveal how that place promotes peace.
  • Read carefully as each place has a tip you can use in your own life.
  • Visit all locations to complete your peace journey.
