Key Takeaways

Aspect Detail
Community Heroes Different leaders like Chiefs, Teachers, and Elders have special roles in solving community problems.
Dispute Resolution Land and market disputes are often best handled by figures with formal authority, like Chiefs.
Youth Empowerment Youth leaders are vital for mediating peer conflicts and organizing peace clubs.
Wisdom & Unity Elders provide wisdom to calm tensions, while Faith Leaders inspire community unity.

Activity Introduction

Let us meet the Peace Heroes in your community! Each has a special role in keeping people united and solving problems. You will read short scenarios of real-life conflicts or needs, and your task is to choose the Peace Hero who can handle it best. Ready? Let us build your peace team!

Learner Instructions

  1. Read each scenario carefully.
  2. Choose the Peace Hero who would be best suited to help in that situation.
  3. Use what you know about each hero’s role in the community.
  4. Read the feedback after each choice. It will help you think like a true peacebuilder!
