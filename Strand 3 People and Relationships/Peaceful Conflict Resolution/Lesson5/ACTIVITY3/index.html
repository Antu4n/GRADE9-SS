Let us see how emotionally smart you really are! You will face real-life moments that can lead to conflict. Your mission? Choose the response that shows emotional intelligence. Ready to think with both your heart and your head? Let us go!

Learner Instructions

  5. Continue until you have completed all the scenarios.
