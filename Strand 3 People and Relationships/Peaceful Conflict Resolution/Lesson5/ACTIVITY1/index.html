Activity Introduction

Let us become peace detectives! Communities use amazing ideas to prevent conflict and promote harmony. Your mission: match each initiative to what it is meant to do. Ready? I am Tana and let us decode peace in action!

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the peace initiative cards on one side of the screen.
  2. Read the purpose cards on the other side.
  3. Drag each peace initiative to the purpose that best matches what it is meant to do in the community.
  4. After each match, read the feedback to check your thinking.
  5. If your match is not correct, use the feedback to guide you and try again until all pairs are matched.
