Activity Introduction

Peace does not just happen, it is planned! Imagine your community wants to stay united and prevent conflict. It is up to you to help design a smart, peaceful path. Drag each step into the correct order to build a peace plan that brings everyone together.

Learner Instructions
  1. Review all the peacebuilding actions in random order.
  2. Drag and arrange them into the sequence you believe works best.
  3. Think logically, what must happen first before the next step can work?
  4. Submit your sequence for feedback.
  5. If any steps are misplaced, use the guidance to adjust and try again.
Here is a Hint
  • Start with the foundation, then build toward action and ongoing maintenance.
