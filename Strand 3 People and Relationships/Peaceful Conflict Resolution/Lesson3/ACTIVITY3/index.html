Activity Introduction

Sometimes, emotions can take over and make us act without thinking. But not today! In this challenge, you will face real-life situations where your feelings could lead to conflict, or to peace. Your job is to spot the emotion and choose the smartest, calmest response. Are you ready to prove you are a true Emotion Hero? Let us go!

Learner Instructions

  1. Read carefully each short real-life scenario.
  2. Notice the main emotion in the situation, anger, sadness, fear, or frustration.
  3. Look at the three possible responses on your screen and click the one you believe is the calmest and most peaceful choice.
  4. After you choose, read the feedback. It will help you understand why it works or guide you to try again.
  5. Use the Hint button if you feel stuck. It will give you a clue about how to manage that specific emotion.
