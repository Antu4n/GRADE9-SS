Activity Introduction

Big emotions need smart strategies! In this activity, your mission is to match each emotion with a calm, down technique that helps manage it peacefully. Whether it is anger, sadness, or fear, there is always a peaceful way to respond. Drag each emotion to the strategy that works best. Let us get matching!

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the emotion cards on your screen.
  2. Look at the calm-down strategy cards on the other side.
  3. Drag each emotion to the strategy that you think will help manage it best.
  4. After you make a match, read the feedback carefully. It will tell you why it works or guide you to try again.
  5. Use the 💡 Hint button if you need help.
  6. Think about what each emotion feels like and what would make it better.
  7. Keep matching until every emotion has a strategy partner.
