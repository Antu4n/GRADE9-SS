Activity Introduction

Let us take a tour through the world of feelings! Emotions can rise quickly like a storm, but when we learn to name them and understand them, we can guide them in a peaceful direction. In this activity, you will explore six important emotions that show up during conflict. Click on each one to uncover what it means, when it appears, and how to stay in control. Let us power up your emotional wisdom!
