Activity Introduction

Whoa! Look at that wall. It is not made of bricks. It is made of things that block peace! Every brick hides a barrier that gets in the way of solving problems calmly. But do not worry you have got the power to break it down. Each time you click a brick, you will uncover a challenge and learn how to overcome it like a true peace hero. Let us start smashing those barriers!

Learner Instructions

  • Look at the wall of Barriers to Peace.
  • Click on each glowing brick to reveal a barrier to peace.
  • Each click unlocks a new piece of peace wisdom!

Exploration Summary

Barrier How to Overcome
Anger Take slow, deep breaths or count to 10 before responding.
Lack of Communication Use clear, kind words and make time to listen to the other person’s side.
Pride Remember that saying "I was wrong" is a strength, not a weakness.
Fear Choose a safe, calm moment to speak and find someone who will listen respectfully.
Peer Pressure Be confident in your values, say no when something feels unfair or unkind.
Prejudice or Bias Get to know people as individuals before forming opinions.
Blame Shifting Focus on your own actions first, ask, "What can I do to help fix this?"
Bottled-Up Emotions Share your feelings calmly with someone you trust before they build up.
Misunderstanding Ask questions to clarify before reacting, "Did you mean...?"
Impatience Slow down and give time for everyone to share their thoughts.
