Activity Introduction

Sometimes, emotions can lead us straight into conflict. But what if you had a plan, a peaceful plan, to guide your response? In this activity, you will build your own Peace Plan using emotional intelligence. At each step, choose what to do, and discover how small actions can make a big difference!

Learner Instructions

  1. Read the question at each step in the Peace Plan.
  2. Choose one option from the ones on screen.
  3. If you choose the most emotionally intelligent response, you will move to the next step.
  4. If your choice is not the best, you will get feedback and a chance to try again.
  5. Complete all four steps to build your personalised Peace Plan.

Key Takeaways

  • ✓
    Emotional intelligence involves recognizing and managing your own emotions and understanding others' emotions (empathy).
  • ✓
    Applying Emotional Intelligence helps in making good decisions during conflicts.
  • ✓
    Emotions like anger, fear, and frustration are normal, but they need managing.
  • ✓
    Emotional intelligence promotes respect for diversity and standing up for what is right.
  • ✓
    Empathy and peaceful actions build stronger, kinder communities.
  • ✓
    You can build a Peace Plan and use it in real-life situations to stay calm, listen, and solve problems together.
