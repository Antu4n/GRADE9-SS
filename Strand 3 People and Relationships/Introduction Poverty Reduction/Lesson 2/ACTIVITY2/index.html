Activity Introduction

One action can set off a whole chain of events, just like a line of falling dominoes. When we take too much from nature, the effects ripple through the environment, our food supply, our jobs, and our communities. In this activity, you will put the events in the right order to show how overusing natural resources can lead to poverty. Watch the chain unfold as you connect each step. Ready to see how it all links together?

Learner Instructions
  1. Read all the event blocks on the side of your screen.
  2. Drag and drop each block into the correct spot in the chain.
  3. Follow the logical order, from environmental damage to human impact.
  4. Rearrange if needed until the chain is complete.
Here is a Hint
  • Nature changes first, which then affects our ability to produce food.
  • Consider how reduced food production impacts jobs and income.
  • Poverty is the final outcome of these compounding challenges.
