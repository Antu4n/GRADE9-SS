Activity Introduction

Poverty reduction has powerful ripple effects like dropping a stone in a pond. Some benefits are felt first by families and individuals, while others lift up the whole society. Your mission? Sort each outcome card into the right category. Think carefully, who feels the impact most directly?

Learner Instructions

  1. Drag each outcome card into the basket where it belongs: Individual/Family Benefit or Community/Society Benefit .
  2. Think about who feels the change first, is it a single person or household, or the whole community?
  3. Drop the card in the correct category and see instant feedback to guide your thinking.
  4. Keep sorting until every card finds its home!
