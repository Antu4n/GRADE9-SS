Activity Introduction

Let us play cause detective! Poverty does not just appear; it has many roots. Your challenge is to sort the causes into three groups: Economic, Social, or Political. Drag each card into the right column based on what it affects; money, people’s lives, or leadership. Think carefully. Each choice brings us closer to understanding poverty!

Learner Instructions

Drag each item from the list into the correct category. Think:

  • Economic: Is it about money or jobs?
  • Social: Does it affect people’s well-being or daily life?
  • Political: Does it involve leadership or laws?
