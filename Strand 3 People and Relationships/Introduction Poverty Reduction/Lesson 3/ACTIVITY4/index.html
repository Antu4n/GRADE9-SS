Activity Introduction

Ready to be an innovator? In this activity, you will become a solution designer. You will go Step-By-Step to solve a poverty-related challenge using your creativity, thinking, and teamwork. Drag and drop ideas, evaluate them, and pick the best solution. Let us build something amazing together!

Learner Instructions

  1. Pick a problem: Choose a poverty-related challenge that matters most to you.
  2. Brainstorm solutions: Come up with at least 2–3 creative ideas.
  3. Evaluate your ideas: Rate each idea for cost, impact, and sustainability.
  4. Select the best solution: Choose your top idea and explain why.
  5. Share it: Submit your idea so others can be inspired!
