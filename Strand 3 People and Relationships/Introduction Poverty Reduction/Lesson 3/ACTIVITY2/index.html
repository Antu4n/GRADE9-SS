Key Takeaways

Aspect Detail
Sustainable Impact Teaching skills and spreading knowledge creates lasting, long-term benefits for the entire community.
Income Security Choosing projects that provide consistent, year-round yields are often more reliable than seasonal ones.
Community Care Balancing income generation with compassion for those in need strengthens social bonds and community health.
Shared Benefits Investing in essential needs like clean water or shared tools benefits the collective group rather than just individuals.

Activity Introduction

Across Kenya, everyday heroes are transforming their communities through smart, practical actions. You will meet youth groups, women's cooperatives, and others who turn challenges into opportunities. Step into their shoes, explore their ideas, and decide the best next move to reduce poverty where they live.

Learner Instructions

  1. Read the background story for each local hero or community group.
  2. Think about their goals, challenges, and resources.
  3. Choose the option you believe will have the greatest long-term impact on reducing poverty.
  4. Read the feedback to see how your choice compares to the possible outcomes.
  5. Complete all 5 scenarios!
