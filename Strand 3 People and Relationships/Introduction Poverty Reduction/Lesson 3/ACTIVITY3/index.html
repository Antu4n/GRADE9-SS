Activity Introduction

Ready to solve real problems using your creativity? Each puzzle piece on the board represents a challenge that leads to poverty. Drag a piece to the centre and unlock a creative mission! Your goal: come up with a powerful local solution using what you know, see, and imagine in your community. Let us build a better future one idea at a time.

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the puzzle pieces, each one shows a poverty-related challenge.
  2. Drag one puzzle piece to the centre of the board to reveal its question.
  3. Read the challenge and choose the best solution from the four options.
  4. If you are not sure, think about local resources, skills, and teamwork before answering.
  5. Complete all puzzle pieces to finish the activity and see your final feedback.
