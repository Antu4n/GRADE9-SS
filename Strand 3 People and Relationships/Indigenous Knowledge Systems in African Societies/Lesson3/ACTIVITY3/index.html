Let us compare two powerful knowledge systems: Indigenous and Modern. Both have helped people solve problems, grow food, and protect health but in different ways. Your task is to drag each statement to the correct section of the Venn diagram: Is it unique to Indigenous Knowledge, Modern Knowledge, or do they overlap? Let us discover how they connect!
