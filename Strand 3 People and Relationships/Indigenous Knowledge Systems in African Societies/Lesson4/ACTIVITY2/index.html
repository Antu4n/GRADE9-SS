Wisdom Journal

Activity Introduction

Throughout this unit, you have explored how Indigenous Knowledge Systems shaped African societies, through farming, healing, weather prediction, peacebuilding, and more. Now it is your turn to reflect. How has this knowledge inspired you? What values will you carry forward?

Learner Instructions

  1. Read each journal prompt carefully.
  2. Write your responses in full sentences, using your own thoughts and examples.
  3. Try to connect your answers to what you have learnt in this unit about Indigenous Knowledge Systems.
  4. Be honest and reflective, there are no wrong answers, but thoughtful responses will help you appreciate these systems more deeply.
  5. Complete all four prompts.

Here is a hint

Think about something you have seen, heard, or learnt that relates to your culture or community. How does it help people live well together or with the environment?
