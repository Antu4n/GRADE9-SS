Activity Introduction

Let us go on a discovery quest! Across Africa, communities used special knowledge systems to live, stay healthy, grow food, and stay united. In this challenge, you will match different Indigenous Knowledge Systems with their correct descriptions and real-life uses. Think of it like being a cultural detective, observe, reflect, and connect the pieces.

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the list of Indigenous Knowledge Systems below.
  2. Drag each one to match it with the correct description and real-life example.
  3. If you are not sure, try your best; you will get hints to guide you.
  4. Keep matching until all pairs are correct.
  5. Think about how each knowledge system connects to life in communities today.
