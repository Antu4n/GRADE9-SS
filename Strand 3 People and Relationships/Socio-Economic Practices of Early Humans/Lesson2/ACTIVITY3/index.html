Key Takeaways

Aspect Detail
Tool Specialization Early humans developed specific tools like hand axes, scrapers, and digging sticks for distinct tasks.
Survival Skills Choosing the right tool was essential for hunting, gathering, and preparing food efficiently.
Problem Solving Stone Age people had to think carefully about materials and shapes to achieve their goals.
Technological Progress The invention of specialized tools marked a huge step forward in early human history.

Activity Introduction

"Welcome, apprentice! Your tribe needs your help to survive. You will face real-life situations from the Stone Age and must choose the best tool for each job. Think carefully, the right choice keeps the tribe safe, the wrong one wastes time and effort. Let us see if you have got what it takes!"

Learner Instructions

  1. Read each scenario carefully.
  2. Look at the list of tool options.
  3. Choose the one that best fits the task.
  4. After each choice, read the feedback to check your thinking.
  5. Answer all scenarios to complete the challenge.
