Activity Introduction

Get ready to show what you have learnt! This is a hand axe, one of the most important tools in early human history.

Your job is to label its parts by dragging each name to where it belongs. Look closely at the shape and imagine how it was held and used. Let us begin!

Learner Instructions

  • Look closely at the diagram of the hand axe.
  • Drag each label to the part of the tool where you think it belongs.
  • Watch for feedback if your choice does not seem right. You can try again until all labels are correct.
  • When all parts are labelled correctly, your progress bar will fill to 100%.
