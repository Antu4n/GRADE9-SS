Activity Introduction

What makes a community feel alive, safe, and welcoming? In this activity, you will explore a vibrant, thriving community. Your challenge is to look closely and identify the visible signs of strong, healthy relationships. Click on different areas in the picture to uncover how teamwork, respect, and care help communities grow and succeed. Let us discover the true impact of healthy relationships!

Learner Instructions

  • Look closely at the thriving community scene on your screen.
  • Click on the highlighted areas that may represent positive relationship outcomes.
  • Read and listen to the explanation that appears to understand how that action or scene benefits the community.
  • Explore all areas until you have uncovered every example of healthy relationship impact.
