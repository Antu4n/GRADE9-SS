Activity Introduction

You have seen how healthy relationships strengthen families, schools, neighbourhoods, and even nations. Now it is time to put your skills to the test. You will be given real-life scenarios. For each one, choose the action that best shows how to build strong and positive connections. Let us see how you would make a difference in your community.

Learner Instructions

  1. Read each scenario carefully.
  2. Think about which choice will strengthen relationships and improve the situation.
  3. Click your answer.
  4. If correct, you will see an explanation of why it works.
  5. If incorrect, read the feedback and try again.
