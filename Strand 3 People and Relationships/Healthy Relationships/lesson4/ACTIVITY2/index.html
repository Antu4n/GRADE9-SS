Activity Introduction

Relationships are like threads in a web. When they are strong and respectful, they connect every part of our community. In this activity, you will match examples of healthy relationship behaviours to the part of the community where they fit best. From family to nation, let us see how kindness, respect, and teamwork create strong and united communities.

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the community web diagram with Healthy Relationships in the centre and four outer circles: Family, School, Neighbourhood, Nation.
  2. Read each behaviour label carefully.
  3. Drag the label to the community level where it best fits.
  4. If your choice is correct, you will see an explanation.
  5. If it is not correct, read the hint in the feedback and try again.
  6. Match all labels to complete your web.
