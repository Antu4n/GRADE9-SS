Activity Introduction

Sometimes even the best teams face disagreements. But here is your chance to step in and help. You will face real-life conflict situations involving classmates. Your goal is to choose how to respond and see how your choice affects the outcome. Some choices lead to peace and understanding, others may make things worse. Ready to become a relationship repair expert? Let us begin!

Learner Instructions

  1. Read the scenario carefully to understand what is happening and who is involved.
  2. Think about the problem in the situation, what is causing tension or hurt feelings?
  3. Review the three possible responses and consider which one would lead to the most respectful and fair outcome.
  4. Click your chosen response to see what happens.
  5. Read the feedback to learn why your choice worked well or how it could be improved.
  6. Continue through all the scenarios to explore different situations and practice making positive relationship choices.
