Activity Introduction

Let us gear up with your very own strategy toolkit! On one side, you will see common barriers that cause problems in relationships. On the other hand, you will find powerful strategies that help solve those problems. Your challenge is to drag the best strategy to match each barrier. Think of it like solving a puzzle.

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the list of barriers on the left side of your screen.
  2. Read each barrier carefully and think about what problem it causes in a relationship.
  3. Drag the strategy card from the right side that best solves the problem.
  4. Check the feedback after each match to see why it is correct or how to improve your choice.
  5. Keep matching until all barriers have the right strategies in place.
