Activity Introduction

Conflict is a normal part of life, but how we handle it makes all the difference. In this activity, you will build a smart and peaceful path for resolving disagreements. At each step, you will choose what to do. Your goal? Turn conflict into cooperation. Let us begin!

Learner Instructions
  1. Read the situation at the top of the screen carefully.
  2. At each step in the flowchart, choose the action you think is the best way to move toward a peaceful resolution.
  3. If you choose correctly, you will move to the next step in the flow.
  4. If your choice is not the best, you will get feedback and a chance to try again.
  5. Keep going until you reach "Resolution Achieved!"
Here is a Hint
  • Think about what will keep the conversation respectful, fair, and calm.

Activity Conclusion

You have just built a peaceful path to resolving conflict. By staying calm, listening carefully, sharing your feelings, and finding fair solutions, you have shown real maturity and empathy. Keep using these steps every day to turn disagreements into opportunities for growth and understanding.
