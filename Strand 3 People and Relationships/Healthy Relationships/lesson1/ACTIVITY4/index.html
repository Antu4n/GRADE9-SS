Activity Introduction

Let us see how you handle real-life relationship moments! You will read short scenarios and choose the most respectful, caring, and trust-building response. Think about how each choice might make the other person feel. Ready? Let us begin!

Learner Instructions

  1. Read each scenario carefully.
  2. Choose the response that best builds trust, respect, and understanding.
  3. After answering, review the feedback to learn why it is correct or what you could improve.
  4. Try to make the best choice on your first attempt, but remember, learning happens with reflection too!
