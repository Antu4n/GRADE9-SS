Activity Introduction

Let us power up your relationship skills! In this activity, you will explore two key ingredients that help relationships grow stronger, trust and appreciation. Trust helps people feel safe and rely on each other. Appreciation is how we show others that we value their help and kindness. You will see a list of actions on the screen. Your mission: drag each action from the bottom to the correct category above. Let us get sorting!

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the two category bins at the top of the screen: Trust Building and Showing Appreciation.
  2. Read each action card at the bottom carefully.
  3. Drag and drop the card from the bottom into the bin where it best fits.
  4. After each attempt, read the feedback to understand your choice.
  5. Keep sorting until all actions are correctly matched.
