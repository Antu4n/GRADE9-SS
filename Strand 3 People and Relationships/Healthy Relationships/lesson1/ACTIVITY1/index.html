Activity Introduction

Ready to boost your relationship-building superpowers? In this interactive match-up challenge, you will pair everyday actions, like listening or saying thank you, with the powerful values they represent. Just drag each behaviour to the concept it best fits. Watch out, you will need to think deeply and make smart connections. Let us get matching!

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the behaviours listed on the screen.
  2. Look at the relationship concepts cards.
  3. Drag each behaviour tile to the concept card that you think it matches best.
  4. After each match, you will receive feedback to help you understand your choice.
  5. Keep matching until all behaviours are correctly paired with their concepts.
  6. If you are unsure, think about what value or skill that behaviour demonstrates in a relationship.
