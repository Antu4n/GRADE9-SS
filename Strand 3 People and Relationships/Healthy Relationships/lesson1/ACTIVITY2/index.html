Activity Introduction

Let us sharpen your social skills with a fun challenge! On the screen, you will see a lively school scene filled with different interactions. Some learners are showing excellent relationship skills while others might be acting in ways that cause problems. Your mission is to tap on each part of the picture and decide whether what you see is part of a healthy relationship or not.

Learner Instructions

  • Look carefully at the illustrated school scene.
  • Click or tap on each character interaction to reveal an explanation.
  • Read why the behaviour is healthy or unhealthy.
  • Explore all the hotspots to complete the challenge.
