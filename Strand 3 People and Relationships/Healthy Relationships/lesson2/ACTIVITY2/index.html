Activity Introduction

It is time to become a relationship expert! On your screen, you will see words that describe different behaviours and attitudes. Some of these are barriers that block healthy relationships, others are helpful and supportive. Drag each item into the correct bin. Can you sort out what brings people together and what drives them apart?

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at each behaviour or attitude card shown on your screen.
  2. Decide if it is a Barrier to Healthy Relationships or Not a Barrier.
  3. Drag the card into the correct bin.
  4. Read the feedback after each drop to understand why your choice is correct or needs changing.
  5. Keep sorting until all cards are in the right bin.

Key Takeaways

Concept Detail
Barriers to Healthy Relationships Behaviours like jealousy, rumours, and disrespect that cause conflict, break trust, and damage harmony.
Healthy Behaviours Actions like honesty, good communication, and inclusion that bring people closer and build strong relationships.
