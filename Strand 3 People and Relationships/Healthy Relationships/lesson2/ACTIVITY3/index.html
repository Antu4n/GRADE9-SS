Activity Introduction

It is time to put your barrier-spotting skills to the test! In each scenario, you will see a real-life situation from school, home, or the community. Your mission is to spot the behaviour that acts as a barrier to healthy relationships. Read carefully, think about how the people might feel, and choose wisely. Let us begin!

Learner Instructions

  1. Read the scenario carefully and imagine yourself in that situation.
  2. Think about how the people might feel in the scene.
  3. Select the behaviour that acts as a barrier to building a healthy relationship.
  4. After your choice, read the feedback to understand why it is correct or incorrect.
  5. Continue through all the scenarios to sharpen your barrier-spotting skills.
