Activity Introduction

Let us build a population pyramid piece by piece! Your job is to drag each part of the pyramid, like the title, age groups, gender sides, and labels, to its correct place on the diagram.
Think carefully! Where do males usually appear? What about age groups? When a piece fits, it will snap into place.
Ready to become a pyramid pro? Let us get started!

Learner Instructions

  • Drag each pyramid part, title, age group labels, gender sides, and population scale, to its correct position on the diagram.
  • Watch for the snap when it is in the right place, and check the feedback to learn as you go.
  • Complete the puzzle to reveal a full, working population pyramid!
