    Key Takeaways

    ConceptDetail
    Age-Sex PyramidVisually represents a population's structure by age and gender.
    ComponentsHorizontal bars (population size), Vertical axis (age groups), Left (males), Right (females), Title, Labels.
    Shape InterpretationBroad base = High proportion of young; Narrow base = Lower proportion of young.
    Bulges & TopsBulge = Large cohort in that age group; Top width = Number of elderly.
