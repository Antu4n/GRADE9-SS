Activity Introduction

It is time to compare two very different population stories, Kenya and Germany. Can you spot what makes each country unique? You will see a list of population characteristics. Drag each one into the correct column: Kenya or Germany. Think about what you have learnt about birth rates, age groups, and dependency ratios.

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at the population characteristics at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Drag each characteristic into the correct country column, Kenya or Germany.
  3. Drop the tiles where they fit best and check the feedback to see if you are on the right track.
  4. Sort all items correctly to complete the comparison!

Key Takeaways

Indicator Observation
Birth Rate High rates lead to youthful populations (Kenya); Low rates lead to ageing populations (Germany).
Death Rate High rates reduce population size and life expectancy; Low rates contribute to longer lives and ageing (Germany).
Migration Movement of people significantly impacts the age and size of populations in both origin and destination countries.
Summary Kenya and Germany differ because of their respective stages of economic and social development.
