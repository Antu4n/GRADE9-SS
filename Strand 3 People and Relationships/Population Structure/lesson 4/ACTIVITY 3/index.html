Activity Introduction

Welcome to the National Planning Committee of Demographica. Your country has a youthful population structure. First, analyze the pyramid to understand who you are planning for. Then, you will be given a limited budget of 100 units to distribute across five key sectors. Aim to find the perfect balance to secure the future!

Learner Instructions

  1. Notice the wide base of the population pyramid. Think about what demographic needs the most resources.
  2. Use the sliders to allocate your 100 budget units.
  3. Release the slider to submit your allocation for that sector. If it falls within the ideal range, it will lock in!
  4. Successfully allocate all five sectors to complete the challenge.
