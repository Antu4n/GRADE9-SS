Activity Introduction

Time to step into the shoes of a national planner! You will see a set of population scenarios like a growing number of babies or a large elderly population. Your job? Drag and drop the most appropriate resource or service to match each scenario. Think carefully about who needs what, and how population structure affects national planning. Let us get started!

Learner Instructions

  1. Look at each scenario card on your screen. Each card shows a population situation (e.g., many babies, more elderly people, rising unemployment).
  2. Review the available resource tiles at the bottom. Examples: Schools & Teachers, Pensions & Elder Care, Job Centres & Skills Training, Infant Health & Nutrition Services, Hospitals & Clinics.
  3. Drag and drop the resource tile you think best solves the scenario onto the matching card.
  4. Check the feedback after each match.
  5. Match all scenarios correctly to complete the activity and see the final summary of how population structure guides national planning.
