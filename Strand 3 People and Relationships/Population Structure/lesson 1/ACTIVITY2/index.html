Activity Introduction

Welcome to Data Dive, where we trace the journey of population data from your neighbourhood all the way to the people in charge! In this activity, you will click through the steps of a real data flow, from the moment it is collected during a census or birth registration, to when it is cleaned and organized, and finally to when it is shared in reports and dashboards. Each step is like a link in a chain and every link is important.

Learner Instructions
  1. Click each stage in the flowchart to follow the journey of population data, from collection, to processing, to publication, and finally to its use in decision-making.
  2. Explore how each step transforms raw facts into valuable information. Complete all stages to reveal the full data trail!
Here is a Hint
  • Start from the very first step at the top.
  • Click the highlighted, active step to reveal the information.
  • You must unlock the current step to access the next one.
